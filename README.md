# Greenfield Market - Local Community Store

A warm, community-focused online store built with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, designed to support local businesses and foster community connections.

## Community Features

- **Local Products**: Fresh bread, farm eggs, honey, and handmade goods
- **Community Information**: Store hours, location, and contact details  
- **Local Delivery**: Support for both pickup and local delivery options
- **Personal Touch**: Family-owned store with 40 years of community service
- **Responsive Design**: Works seamlessly on all devices
